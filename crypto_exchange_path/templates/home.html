What is CryptoFeeSaver?

CryptoFeeSaver is a search engine that computes deposit, trading and withdrawal fees of the top exchanges to find the cheapest path for each operation

Most people just compare trading fees when choosing an exchange site, but they overlook the huge impact that deposit and withdrawal fees can have in the whole operation. Indeed, the latter can usually have a greater impact for low and medium size operations.

You don't want to find yourself trapped in an exchange that charges you an outrageous amount for withdrawing your funds, and this happens everyday. Our end-to-end search engine allows you to estimate the fees for the whole operation so that you can always make the right decision.

The paths proposed by the search engine vary considerably depending on the amount and coins selected, so we encourage you to check our engine for every operation.
